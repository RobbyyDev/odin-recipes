The veggie burger recipe

With this black bean burger recipe, you will never want to eat frozen veggie burgers again! These are a quick, easy, and flavorful vegetarian delight.

Ingredients Required :

  • Vegetables
  • Seasonings
  • Egg
  • Bread crumbs

How to make the Veggie Burger Step-By-Step :

  1. Mash the beans and chop the remaining vegetables and garlic in the food processor.
  2. Stir the veggies into the beans and combine the seasonings and egg in a separate bowl.
  3. MMix in the bread crumbs and divide the mixture into patties.
  4. Grill the patties for about 8 minutes on each side.
